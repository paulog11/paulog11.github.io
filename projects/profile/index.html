About

A bit about me.

I'm a software engineer based in Tokyo, Japan. I enjoy creating solutions for life's daily frustrations for myself and for others.

At work, I am depended on to take care of any and all tasks. Managing and maintaining legacy code, debugging obscure issues, starting new projects, and mentoring younger developers. Of course, I enjoy learning about the cutting edge, but I also find joy in getting deep into established software.

Outside of work, I'm often volunteering for a non-profit organization called International Student Mentor Program. There, I leverage my skills to provide solutions for our mentors and the students that we serve. I also enjoy reading books, watching and playing sports, going fishing, doing photography, being out in nature, and eating good food.
